Metric definitions
  • Day-43 retention (new · NSM) — of an install-day cohort, the share still active on day 43. Denominator = all installs. Plotted by day-43 date.
  • First-paid conversion (new · driver) — of new installs, the share making their first paid recharge within 7 days of free-trial expiry.
  • Expiry-day renewals (new · driver) — of new-customer paid-plan expiries (all plans blended), the share whose next plan starts on or before expiry.
  • Renewal-payment success (new · input) — of new-customer renewal-payment checkouts, the share reaching payment success within 5 minutes (attempts deduped per 5-min).
  • App-open near expiry (new · input) — of new-customer expiries, the share where the customer opened the app in the 3 days before expiry.
  • Active-base retention (tenured · NSM) — active tenured customers today ÷ active tenured 30 days ago (rolling 30-day ratio). MTD = of the base active on the 1st, share still active yesterday.
  • On-time renewal (tenured · driver) — of tenured paid-plan expiries (all plans), the share whose next plan starts on or before expiry.
  • Grace recovery (tenured · driver) — of tenured expiries that missed on-time renewal, the share who recharge within 15 days (before churning out). Matures 15 days after expiry.
  • On paid plan (both · guardrail) — of the recently-relevant base (a paid plan live, or expired ≤30 days), the share currently on a live paid plan; the remainder splits into lapsed 0–7 days and lapsed 7+ days (the at-risk tail). New cohort counts only customers with ≥1 paid recharge.
  • % active days (tenured · guardrail) — average share of days the connection was active over the rolling 30 days.
  • 1-day plan % (tenured · guardrail) — 1-day recharges ÷ all paid recharges (lower is better for LTV).
Method
  • Base = all Home broadband (store_group_id=0), every plan type (PAYG, legacy, migrated). Standard trum filters, IST. Only split is tenure (new <43d / tenured).
  • Active = plan live at the checkpoint or last plan ended within 15 days.
  • Lagged metrics exclude the current, still-maturing day/cohort. Windows roll on the last 30 days. Charts use a fixed 0–100% axis.
  • Refreshed daily from Metabase. Not yet cross-checked against an existing Metabase dashboard.
Both NSMs toggle daily / weekly. Tenured is a 30-day rolling ratio; its earliest daily points sit on a small base.
